The image shows a sample of what the first decision stage will look like. You will be able to interact with the entire data set in the graph later on. Again, this is the same and only data set the ML algorithm can make use of. Also, as mentioned before, the time series you see comes from real demand data of a product. You will eventually enter a forecast in the box at the bottom of the screen and advance pressing the "Next" button.
In the second decision stage the screen will look similar, only then the ML algorithm's forecast is presented as well. In this stage you will enter your final forecast and move on with the "Next" button.

After passing both decision stages, the prediction of one time step (one day) is done. The prediction procedure then starts over and you will go through both decision stages again to make a forecast for the next time step. This will be repeated for all 30 time steps.
